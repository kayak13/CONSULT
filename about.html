About the Practice

My work sits between lived experience and structured review. I’m not a law firm, and I don’t give legal advice. I function as a documentation and systems-focused specialist when administrative processes have gone off the rails.

Many people meet me when they are already exhausted: they have called, emailed, and explained their situation repeatedly, only to be told that “nothing can be done” or that their case will be “reviewed” with no clear timeline. At that point, the missing element is rarely one more phone call—it is a clear record.

I help you build that record: what happened, when, who said what, which documents exist, and how that contrasts with written policy or standard process where applicable. The result is a narrative that can be understood by oversight bodies, supervisors, or future advocates.
