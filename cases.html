Case Examples

Details are anonymized and outcomes are not guaranteed. These examples simply illustrate the type of work this practice takes on.

Case 01 — The Vanishing Referral

Multiple referrals to a specialist were marked as “sent,” but never reached the receiving office. The client was repeatedly told to “check back later.”

  • Rebuilt the timeline from portal messages and call logs
  • Identified where handoffs failed between systems
  • Drafted a concise, factual summary for clinic leadership

Case 02 — Billing vs. Expectations

A client received a bill and collection threat that did not match how services had been described at intake.

  • Compared intake paperwork, agreements, and invoices
  • Flagged inconsistencies in documentation and coding
  • Helped the client submit a written dispute and request review

Case 03 — “Policy” as a Wall

An organization denied continued access to a program, citing “policy” without providing clear explanation or written criteria.

  • Obtained and reviewed the written policy
  • Documented differences between text and practice
  • Supported the client in requesting a formal review
