Core Focus Areas

Insurance & Medical Breakdown Review

When referrals, authorizations, or coverage decisions stall, disappear, or contradict what you were told. I map who did what, when, and where process failed or communication broke down.

Billing, Claims & Collections Strategy

For balances, denials, or collection threats that don’t align with your understanding of the services provided. I review the paper trail and help you respond in writing.

Policy & Process Failure Analysis

When “policy” is used as a shield for actions that don’t line up with written rules or basic fairness. I compare policy language to what actually occurred and help you request review or correction.

Who This Is Not For

To maintain quality and protect your time, this practice is selective. The following situations are generally not a fit.

  • Individuals seeking legal representation, legal advice, or an attorney–client relationship.
  • Situations requiring immediate emergency action (same-day deadlines).
  • Matters primarily driven by emotional dispute rather than documentation or policy.
  • Individuals unwilling to gather or share the documents needed for a proper review.
  • “General advocacy” requests that do not involve a clear process, program, or policy breakdown.
  • Anyone expecting guaranteed outcomes or demanding results outside my control.

If your matter does not align with this scope, you will be notified early so you do not waste time.
