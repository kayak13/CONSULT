Pricing & Packages

Pricing varies by complexity, volume of documentation, and urgency. These ranges are provided so you can understand the general scale of work.

Case Snapshot

For single-issue situations.

  • Limited document review
  • Timeline and issue summary
  • Suggested next steps you can take independently

Best for early-stage concerns or when you primarily need clarity.

System Failure Deep Dive

For layered or ongoing breakdowns.

  • Comprehensive document and correspondence review
  • Process and policy comparison where applicable
  • Draft correspondence and escalation plan

Ideal when multiple departments, decisions, or programs are involved.

Ongoing Support

Reserved for active clients.

  • Priority review of new letters and responses
  • Support for related follow-on issues within the same matter

Available after an initial engagement and subject to capacity.
