Process

The objective is to move you from “I don’t know where to start” to “I know exactly what I’m sending, to whom, and why.”

1

Intake

You complete a short intake form with a description of your situation, any deadlines, and what you’re hoping to achieve.

2

Document Collection

You gather available letters, emails, portal screenshots, and notices. They do not need to be organized; that’s part of the work.

3

Review & Mapping

I reconstruct the timeline, track inconsistencies, and identify where process, policy, or communication appears to have broken down.

4

Strategy & Drafting

You receive a written summary and, depending on the engagement, draft language for correspondence and a plan for next steps.

If your situation falls outside my scope or appears to require immediate legal assistance, I will tell you that rather than accepting the work.
