Services

These services are designed for individuals and small businesses navigating complex, high-frustration situations—not routine customer service issues.

Insurance & Medical Breakdown Review

  • Referrals and prior authorizations that have stalled or vanished
  • Coverage decisions that conflict with earlier guidance
  • Contradictory information between provider, plan, and program

You receive a written summary of what likely occurred and a proposed structure for follow-up, including questions to ask and documents to request.

Billing, Claims & Collections Support

  • Balances or invoices that don’t match your understanding
  • Collection notices tied to disputed or unclear charges
  • Situations where you need a cleaner record before seeking counsel

I help you review statements, timelines, and agreements, then draft clear, factual correspondence reflecting your position.

Compliance & Process Failure Analysis

  • “Policy” cited in ways that feel inconsistent or unfair
  • Programs or services that removed you mid-process
  • Confusing or incomplete written decisions

I highlight process gaps, conflicting information, and potential issues for you to raise through appropriate channels.
